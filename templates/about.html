About MyTodo

MyTodo is a small task catalog built with Flask and SQLite. Every todo is filed like an index card — a title, a description, a priority, and a date stamp marking when it was added.

Use the Home page to add new cards, mark them done, edit details, or pull them from the drawer entirely. The Stats page gives a quick read on how full the drawer is and what's still outstanding.
